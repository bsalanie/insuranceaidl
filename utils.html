utils

Reads the data and formats it.

We start by loading the dataset from the FFSA 1989 survey — only beginning drivers. The data has 6,333 insurees. Each observation has 58 variables:

  1. (AG25) whether the insuree is below 25 (1) or not (2)
  2. (AGE) the age of the insuree in years
  3. (AGEC) the age of the driver in years (9 if the driver was the insuree)
  4. (ALLE) German car: 1 yes, 0 no
  5. (AMER) American car: 1 yes, 0 no
  6. (ANCC) how old the driver license is (1 for less than 3, 2 for more, 9 incomplete, 0 missing)
  7. (CFDO) the nature of the deductible (1 fixed, 2 proportional, 0 no deductible)
  8. (CFVO) whether the theft guarantee comes with a deductible (1 yes, 2 no, 9 not insured against theft)
  9. (CGAR) compulsory insurance (0 is basic, 1 with theft, 2 with fire, 3 =1+2, 4 with windows, 5=1+4, 6=2+4, 7=1+2+4)
  10. (CGDO) comprehensive insurance (0 none, 1 no deductible, 2 with deductible except if collision, 3 with deductible always, 4 others)
  11. (CIDA) percentage at fault for claims smaller than 25,000FF: 0 = 0%, 1=25%, 2=50%, 3=75%, 4=100%, 9=incomplete, 99=missing.
  12. (CITR) Citroën car: 1 yes, 0 no
  13. (CODC) driver is the insured (1 yes, 2 no, 9 ncomplete, 0 missing)
  14. (CODE) accident (0 if none, 30+ if an accident)
  15. (CRES) responsibility (1 total, 2 none, 3 partial, 0 missing, 9 incomplete)
  16. (CSOC) insurance company code
  17. (DCIR) year the car started circulating
  18. (DEPT) département where the car is registered
  19. (DNAI) year of birth of insuree
  20. (FDOS) claim processing code (1 done, 2 in process, 0 missing)
  21. (FRAN) 1 if French car but not Citroën-Peugeot-Renault, 0 otherwise
  22. (GROUP) group: from 1 to 6
  23. (GSIN) guarantee activated by claim (1 compulsory or comprehensive, 2 theft, 3 fire, 4 windows, 9 missing, 0 ???)
  24. (HOM) 1 if insuree is male, otherwise 0
  25. (INDACCI) 1 if no accident at fault, 0 otherwise
  26. (ITAL) Italian car: 1 yes, 0 no
  27. (KVEH) number of cars in claim, including the insuree’s (0 missing, 9 incomplete)
  28. (LIEU) département of claim (99 incomplete, 0 missing)
  29. (MFDO) deductible as percentage of claim value, or as amount if fixed (0 missing)
  30. (MFVO) deductible for theft (0 missing)
  31. (MIDA) reimbursement for claim (0 missing)
  32. (MODR) how the claim was paid (1 interinsurers, 2 no, 0 missing)
  33. (MSIN) the total amount of the claim (0 missing)
  34. (MTRC) the amount covered by the compulsory guarantee (0 missing)
  35. (NATS) the nature of the claim (1 material, 2 personal or both, 0 missing)
  36. (NBACCI) the number of claims at fault
  37. (NORD) an identifier
  38. (NSEQ) another one
  39. (PBAS) the basic premium (= [45]/[51]*100)
  40. (PEUG) Peugeot car: 1 yes, 0 no
  41. (PFIS) car fiscal horsepowers (3 a 8)
  42. (PNET) total premium, after bonus/malus, net of taxes
  43. (POID) percentage of the year covered by insurance
  44. (PROF) occupation (1 shopowner or crafts, 2 salaried, 3 civil servant, 4 farmer, 5 others, 6 retired, 7 other w/o a job, 8 unknown)
  45. (QSIN) number of claims in the year (9 missing)
  46. (RCDR) premium for compulsory coverage after bonus, net of taxes
  47. (REG) region (1 is Bourgogne-Franche Comté, 2 is Bassin Parisien W, 3 is PACA, 4 is Centre W, 5 is Grand W, 6 is SW, 7 is Ile de France, 8 is Rhône-Alpes, 9 is Est, 10 is Nord)
  48. (RENA) Renault car: 1 yes, 0 no
  49. (SCLA) class of car (how expensive, 0 to 4)
  50. (SEXE) gender (1 male, 2 female)
  51. (SGRP) real group of the car (4 to 13)
  52. (TMAJ) bonus/malus (basis 100)
  53. (TRACIR) based on [16]; is 11 if car started circulating before 1978, 10 if in 1978 or 1979, else 1989-[16]
  54. (TRAGE) age category, based on [2]. Is 0 if [2] is 18 or 19, 1 to 4 if 20 to 23, 5 if 24 or 25, 6 if 26 to 30, 7 if 31 to 45, then 8.
  55. (TRAPBAS) category of basic premium, based on [38]. 1 to 6 with breaks at 1,100; 1,400; 1,800; 2,300; and 2,900
  56. (TRES) percentage at fault if shared responsibility (0 missing)
  57. (USAG) main usage of car; 1: work, 2: home-to-work, 3: private use, 4: unknown
